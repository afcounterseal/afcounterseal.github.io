Find a Home for Your "Paper" Vault

Your crypto wallets are protected by 12 or 24 words of seed mnemonics, making up the all-or-nothing total worth of your digital assets. It's time to move them out of the stone age of paper records and store them into the Ai-Fi Incognito Cloud as a Crypton with encryption strength higher than Bitcoins on the blockchain.

Threshold Signature vs. Hardware Wallet

Hardware wallets are perceived secure, as they may be kept in your pants pocket and less susceptible to malware attacks, until they are targeted or lost. Besides, their recovery mnemonic words still need a home. Find out how an all-software Ai-Fi Counterseal solution takes care all your worries.

Slice and Dice Your Secrets

If you insist on using a paper vault, introduce some redundancy to protect those pieces of papers, or mix and match paper with "Cryptons" in the Ai-Fi Incognito Cloud. Split your individual secret into multiple "shares", and distribute them in multiple locations or vaults. Make sure you don't lose the majority of the secret shares.
